Experience The Falls.

Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe is home to the world’s largest waterfall. Considered one of the 7 Wonders of the Natural World, this magnificent site is the centerpiece of this amazing adventure. Trek through the bush on the back of an African Elephant; Search for the Big Five on an exciting night safari; feel the thrill of rafting with Hippos, Crocs, and Elephants on the great Zambezi River; tour one of the greatest feats of engineering in Africa, the Victoria Falls Bridge; and be captivated by the Flight of Angels Helicopter Tour over Victoria Falls!

Trip Details

- 6 days and 6 nights in 4 star accommodations including hot breakfast daily
- Sunset Cruise on the Zambezi River where you’ll see hippos, crocodiles, elephants and more
- Elephant Back Safari
- Night Safari on a “Big 5” reserve
- Soaring helicopter ride over the falls
- Victoria Falls Bridge Tour with magnificent views of the gorge
- High Tea at Victoria Falls Hotel overlooking the Victoria Falls Bridge
- Safari through Zambezi National Park
- Z River ligambeziht raft trip
- BOMA Dinner- An unforgettable cultural experience
